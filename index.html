What is Vitiligo?

Vitiligo is a condition where the skin loses pigment cells, leading to white patches on the skin. What causes white patches
on the skin?

White patches on the skin might indicate Vitiligo, a condition where pigment cells are lost, causing lighter patches. This happens when the immune system attacks melanocytes, which produce skin color. Vitiligo can spread and affect any part of the skin.

Is Vitiligo contagious and
can it spread to others?

No, vitiligo is not contagious and cannot spread to others. It is a condition where the skin loses pigment, resulting in white patches. Homeopathy is one of the best treatment options available to help manage Vitiligo and improve skin appearance.
